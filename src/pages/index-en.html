The Process

1. Submit your brief

Choose the formula that best suits your needs. A few clicks and a few minutes are enough to file your brief. Validate and launch the call for tenders with our community of manufacturers.

2. Receive and compare your offers

Only the manufacturers best able to meet your needs participate in your tender. Relax, Packitoo prepares your file: price offers, manufacturer information, ratings and comments from other users.

3. Order

Select the proposal that suits you best then place an order. You then enter into direct contact with the manufacturer.
